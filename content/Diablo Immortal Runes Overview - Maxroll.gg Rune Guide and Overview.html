Introduction

Runes in Diablo Immortal are used to craft Legendary Gems. There are currently 24 Runes in the game that can be obtained through running Elder Rifts or purchased from the

Fading Ember Merchant in town. Runes fall into 4 different categories which we will discuss in more detail below. Some are used to craft 1 Star Legendary Gems while others are for 2 Star Legendary Gems. 5 Star Legendary Gems can only be crafted using the random recipes at the Jeweler. Getting enough Runes and to craft a 2 Star Legendary Gem is expensive and takes quite awhile. Make sure you're using these resources wisely to rank up a powerful Legendary Gem.

How to Obtain Runes

Elder Rifts

Runes can only be obtained by running Elder Rifts while using a

Rare Crest or
Legendary Crest. Each
Rare Crest provides 4 random Runes and each
Legendary Crest provides 3. On top of this every Elder Rift you run rewards 8
Fading Ember upon completion.

Fading Embers

Fading Ember are resources that drop when completing an Elder Rift. Every week you can obtain up to 320
Fading Ember. Every Elder Rift grants 8 by default and 1 additional for every crest used by your party, up to maximum of 20 per Elder rift. Trade in your
Fading Ember at the Fading Ember Merchant next to the Elder Rift portal in Westmarch. Here you can obtain additional Runes to directly craft the Legendary Gems you desire, you can also trade in for FA which allow you to randomly roll for Legendary Gems at the Jeweler.

The Fading Ember Merchant has 3 categories to choose from. You can either buy FA directly with

Fading Ember Merchant or trade Magic and Rare along with
Fading Ember Merchant for ATI. Check the table to see the crafting costs at the
Fading Ember Merchant.

Crafting Runes

ATI

This is the base Rune used to craft Legendary Gems. 1 Star Legendary Gems require 7 ATI and 2 Star Legendary Gems require 28. You can craft these using your undesired Runes along with

Fading Ember at the
Fading Ember Merchant.

FA

This Rune is very unique in that it isn't used to directly craft any specific Legendary Gem. You can trade in FA at the Jeweler to craft a random 1-5 Star Legendary Gem if desired. There is no other purpose for these so if you choose to purchase these with your

Fading Ember Merchant you'll have to take a gamble on what you can win! All gems received using these recipes can be sold on the Marketplace.

Magic Runes

Magic Runes have a 80% chance to drop and are used to Craft 1 Star Legendary Gems. You can use 2 of the following Magic Runes along with 7 ATI to craft 1 star Legendary Gems. These are the least powerful Legendary Gems in the game and thus require the least to craft. Rare Runes

Rare Runes have a 20% chance to drop and are used to craft 2 Star Legendary Gems. You can use 2 of the following Rare Runes along with 28 ATI to craft 2 star Legendary Gems. 2 Star Legendary Gems are used for quite awhile and can pack a decent punch, so finding Runes and using them to rank up the best ones is highly recommended.

Runes Summary

  • Runes craft 1 and 2 Star Legendary Gems, and have a chance to grant a 5 star Legendary Gem when using the random FA Recipe at the Jeweler.
  • They are found in Elder Rifts or bought from the
    Fading Ember     Merchant    .
  • Magic Runes are much more common than Rare Runes, with the rarest ones crafting the best 2 Star Legendary Gems.
  • Use your
    Fading Ember     wisely to upgrade the best Legendary Gems for your class! Check out our Class Guides to see which ones we recommend.
