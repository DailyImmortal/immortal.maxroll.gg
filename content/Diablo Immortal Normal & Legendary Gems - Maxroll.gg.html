Introduction

Normal Gems and Legendary Gems are found as you journey through Sanctuary, significantly boosting your Attributes and overall power. They may seem underwhelming at first, but their power increases dramatically as you rank them up. Getting high rank Gems is no easy task and requires some time investment to approach max rank for a single Gem. Let's go over what makes each gem in the game unique and how you can best use them to slash through your enemies.

Normal Gems

Normal Gems provide Attribute bonuses based on the Gem type and must be socketed into a slot of their same color on gear. They provide the same bonuses to all classes and are generally prioritized in the same way. There are 3 different colors of gems (Red, Blue and Yellow), each with an offensive and defensive option. They are socketed into your Secondary Gear (Amulets, Rings, Belts, Gloves and Boots). Prioritize Red and Blue Gems when they are offered as a reward, as they are more beneficial than Yellow Gems. Let's take a look at the six different Gems.

How to Obtain Normal Gems

Normal gems primarily are rewarded from doing Bounties and clearing out Hidden Lairs, never skip these activities as the path to high rank gems is a long one! If you see a Hidden Lair while roaming around always hop in and even invite your friends! When in a 4 player party Normal Gems can drop anywhere in the game, when on your own you'll have to rely on the following methods to obtain them:

  • Completing Bounties form the bounty board in Westmarch.
  • Killing Bosses in Hidden Lairs (some lairs have 2 floors granting an extra Boss and Gem!)
  • Using Hilts at the Hilts Merchant
  • Using
    Platinum         at the Marketplace

Ranking Up Normal Gems

Combining three Normal Gems of the same type and rank yields one Gem of the next rank. Ranks beyond 5 also require

Echoing Crystal, which can be purchased from the Jeweler in Westmarch for 500
Platinum.

Red Gems

These are the best Normal Gems in the game. There are no diminishing returns on Damage or Life making the sky the limit! Belts roll with 1-3 red sockets, giving you a reliable place to put your highest rank gems. Amulets and Rings can roll 1 red socket each as well, giving you the possibility of using 6 red gems. Since these gems provide Damage and Life, two of the most important stats, you'll always be pleased to gain another red socket in your gear! Prioritize

Tourmaline over
Ruby since Damage is better than Life.

Legendary Gems

Legendary Gems provide Combat Rating, Resonance as well as strong unique effects. You can equip up to 6 at a time, socketed into your Primary Gear: Main Hand, Off-Hand, Helm, Chest, Shoulders and Pants. These gems increase in power up to rank 10, gaining insane bonuses.

Some of these Legendary Gems are more common than others and they can be categorized by their Star level: 1 star, 2 star and 5 star. All Legendary Gems will unlock another Attribute at Rank 3 with all attributes getting stronger as higher ranks are obtained. When 5 star Legendary Gems are dropped or crafted they come with a random star count between 2-5. As one would expect, 5 star gems are immensely more powerful than 1 and 2 star gems, so whenever you get one of these you'll likely want to work it into your build!

Once rank 10 is reached, Legendary Gems can be used to Awaken Legendary items. Below we go over all the Legendary Gems in the game, check out our Class Guides to see which ones are best suited for each class.

How to Obtain Legendary Gems

  • Completing Elder Rifts while using a
    Rare Crest     or
    Legendary Crest    *
  • Using
    Platinum         at the Marketplace*
  • Craft using Runes and
    Platinum         at Jeweler*
  • Reward from the Battle Pass
  • Using Fading Ember at Fading Ember Merchant
  • Using Hilts at the Hilts Trader

* 5 star gems can only be obtained through these methods.

Legendary Gem Drop Chance

The drop chance and star count of a Legendary Gem vary depending on the type of Crest used in the Elder Rift. For every 50

Legendary Crests used, you are guaranteed to receive one 5 Star Legendary Gem. This guarantee counter is separate from the one for Eternal
Legendary Crest purchased with
Eternal Orbs from the Shop. Here is a breakdown of the data:

Legendary Crest

  • 1 Star: 75.395% Chance to Drop
  • 2 Star: 20.105% Chance to Drop
  • 5 Star: 4.5% Chance to Drop

Rare Crest

  • 1 Star: 5% Chance to Drop

If a 5 Star Gem drops, the following is the chance to roll quality 2-5:

  • 2 Star Quality: 75% Chance to Drop
  • 3 Star Quality: 20% Chance to Drop
  • 4 Star Quality: 4% Chance to Drop
  • 5 Star Quality: 1% Chance to Drop

Ranking Up Legendary Gems

Ranking up Legendary Gems increases their Combat Rating Boost, Resonance bonus as well as their primary effects stats. When getting to Rank 3 Gems unlock another effect and at Rank 5 they grant increased Magic Find. In order to rank up you need to acquire duplicates of the same gem and use Gem Power from breaking down other Legendary Gems. Gem Power comes from breaking down any Legendary Gem, with high star and rank gems giving more of this resource. It's important to keep the Legendary Gems you do obtain, even if they're undesirable to use as this process is quite costly. 1, 2 and 5 star gems all respectively require different amounts of Gem Power in order to rank up, with 5 star gems costing the most to obtain their highest ranks. Any extra Gem Power that is left after an upgrade will turn into

Gem Fragments and be sent to your inventory for later use.

1 Star Legendary Gems

At rank 1 these provide 8 Combat Rating, 15 Resonance and a powerful effect. When ranking up they will gain + 4 Combat Rating, increased Resonance and increased stats to their effect. At Rank 10 they will give 44 Combat Rating. These can be crafted from scratch using Runes, which can be found when doing Elder Rifts with Crests. The overall power of these gems is minimal compared to 2 and 5 star gems, but some of them are decent enough for use until you find better replacements. Crafting these to rank 10 can still be beneficial for Awakening items since they are by far the cheapest to rank up and easiest to obtain.

Crafting at Jeweler

7 - ATI Runes

2 - NIE Runes

This gem might be slightly useful for end game content, but by then you'll have better gems. Replace this gem as soon as possible.

Pain of Subjugation

Crafting at Jeweler

7 - ATI Runes

2 - LUX Runes

Decent damage bonus early on, but requires enemies to be Crowd Controlled. Only use this if you plan on using some form of Crowd Control.

Respite Stone

Reduces all damage you take by 1.6% for every 10% of your Life below maximum, up to a maximum damage reduction of 16%. Gain 6% increased Block Chance while your life is below 50%.

7 - ATI Runes

2 - ORD Runes

Decent damage reduction but there are better 2 and 5 star options.

Seled's Weakening

Gain 18% increased damage for 60 seconds after killing an Elite monster. Increases all damage you deal to Elite monsters by 4%.

Crafting at Jeweler

7 - ATI Runes

2 - TYR Runes

Killing an Elite monster every 60 seconds is easy to pull off in almost all content. This makes it a reliable damage boost and one of the best 2 star gems. Rank it up a bit as you'll likely use it until you find some 5 star gems.

Trickshot Gem

Your Channel Skills consume energy 24% slower. You take 2% decreased damage while using a Channeling Skill.

Crafting at Jeweler

7 - ATI Runes

2 - AUD Runes

Niche gem for builds that focus on a channeling skill like Whirlwind Barbarian. Not great at low rank but worth investing in if you plan on playing a Channel-focused build.

Zod Stone

Increases the duration of Ultimate Skills by 48%. Increased the damage of Ultimate skills by 6%.

Crafting at Jeweler

7 - ATI Runes

2 - URS Runes

Combining this with a high ranked

Ca'arsen's Invigoration may yield interesting results. This combination allows Ultimate's to charge up quickly and last a tremendous amount of time at high rank.

2 Star Legendary Gems

At rank 1 these will provide 12 Combat Rating, 30 Resonance and a powerful effect. When ranking up they will gain + 4 Combat Rating, increased Resonance and increased stats to their effect. At Rank 10 they will give 66 Combat Rating. These can be crafted from scratch using Runes, which can be found when doing Elder Rifts with Crests. Many of these gems have great effects and uses in many game modes. There aren't many 5 star gems in the game currently and finding them is challenging, so ranking up 2 star gems is a great use of your resources.

5 Star Legendary Gems

These are the most powerful Legendary Gems in the game as they provide massive Combat Rating, Resonance and amazing effects. These drop with 2-5 stars unlocked and the number of stars also affects their bonuses. At rank 1 these provide 12-24 Combat Rating and 30-100 Resonance depending on their star count. When ranking up they gain Combat Rating and Resonance based on their Star Count. For example:

  • A Rank 1
    Seeping Bile     with 4 Stars filled in gives 22 Combat Rating and 80 Resonance and will also give +22 Combat Rating for every rank achieved.
  • A Rank 1
    Seeping Bile     with 5 Stars filled in gives 24 Combat Rating and 100 Resonance and will also give +24 Combat Rating for every rank achieved.
  • At Rank 10 with 5 Stars these give 240 Combat Rating.

These gems have a much lower drop chance than their counterparts and cannot be directly crafted at the Jeweler like the lower options. However they can be crafted using the random recipe that is available. The only other ways to obtain these are from doing Elder Rifts or purchasing them from other players on the Marketplace. Whenever you do obtain some you'll almost certainly want to take advantage of their supreme power. Remember that gems drop with a random amount of stars unlocked, so don't make the mistake of ever selling a gem that has all 5 stars unlocked.

Random Recipe Only

A devastating DPS gem for classes that incorporate a loss of control to enemies. One of the strongest Legendary Gems in the game that.

Echoing Shade

Your attacks have a 15% chance to summon a Shadow Clone for 20 seconds that gains some of your abilities. You can have up to 3 shadow clones at a time and their life is increased by 100%.

Crafting at Jeweler

Random Recipe Only

The Shadow Clones use your Primary skills making them only useful on a Demon Hunter. Overall they're quite weak making this one a bit underwhelming. Prioritize other 5 Star Legendary Gems with your resources.

Howler's Call

Primary Attacks have a 10% chance to summon a charging Spirit Wolf that inflicts 360% + 1458 damage to all enemies in its path. 10 second cooldown. The spirit wolf has a 48% chance to stun enemies for 3 seconds. 10 second cooldown.

Crafting at Jeweler

Random Recipe Only

The damage from the Spirit Wolf is noticeable but not overwhelming. This is a decent damage focused gem that can be used in end game if you're able to equip several 5 Star Legendary Gems.

Phoenix Ashes

Prevents fatal damage, and then grants a shield for 6 seconds that absorbs 1050% of your base damage. When the shield expires, heal for 80% of the remaining value. 180 second cooldown.

Crafting at Jeweler

Random Recipe Only

An amazing defensive gem that grows in power as you do. This can turn many risky situations around and is one of the few purely defensive gems in the game.

Seeping Bile

Attacks have a 4% chance to Poison enemies, inflicting 65% + 263 damage every second for 6 seconds and decreases movement speed by 24%. This Poison spreads to nearby enemies if the victim dies. Can only affect each enemy once every 20 seconds.

Crafting at Jeweler

Random Recipe Only

This is one of the strongest Legendary Gems in the game. It is devastating in large densities such as Elder Rifts and Challenge Rifts. Combining this with other damaging Legendary gems like

Blessing of the Worthy and
Blood-Soaked Jade leads to some rapid destruction.

Zwenson's Haunting

When you defeat an enemy, summon a Dark Beast to attack nearby enemies, dealing 120% base damage + 486 to all nearby enemies. You have a 28% chance to summon an additional Dark Beast. 6 second cooldown.

Crafting at Jeweler

Random Recipe Only

This gem is decent for DPS for all classes, it procs more often that most gems but does quite a bit less damage. The Dark Beast also tends to target random mobs in undesired locations. Overall a solid DPS choice that can be a bit inconsistent.

Summary

  • Legendary Gems & Normal Gems are an important part of your character's progression and provide strong effects and Attribute bonuses.
  • Prioritize ranking up Normal Gems in this order Red > Blue > Yellow. Rank them up high and always hop into those Lairs.
  • 1 Star Legendary Gems are used for the early stages of the game and provide small bonuses compared to the others. Cheap to craft and rank up.
  • 2 Star Legendary Gems are decent and will carry your character for most of the game. Expensive to craft and Rank up, worth the investment.
  • 5 Star Legendary Gems provide insane effects and massive Attribute bonuses. Uncraftable and expensive to rank up, always worth the investment.
  • Blood-Soaked Jade    ,
    Blessing of the Worthy     and
    Seeping Bile     are some of the best Legendary Gems available in Diablo Immortal
  • Use your most powerful Legendary Gems & Normal Gems and rank them up to destroy everything in your path.
