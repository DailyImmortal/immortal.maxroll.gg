General

Legendary Items in Diablo Immortal can be Awakened, gaining Resonance to become even more powerful! In this article we cover how to Awaken your items, all the different mechanics involved and guide you towards the most powerful effect for the different classes. Let's begin!

Awakening an Item

To Awaken an item, you must have:

  1. A Legendary Item
    • These can be found everywhere in the Open World, the Battle Pass, in Elder Rifts or from different Events and Quests.
  2. A Rank 10 Legendary Gem socketed inside that item
    • These can be found in Elder Rifts using
      Rare Crest       or
      Legendary Crest      , in the Battle Pass, through Crafting or the Marketplace.
    • The Legendary Gem must be upgraded to its maximum rank of 10 at the Apprentice Jeweler.
  3. A
    Dawning Echo
    • These can only be bought at the in-game shop for 1.000
      Eternal Orbs      .

When you have all of that, visit the Master Jeweler, Vic, on the left side of Westmarch and select the item you want to Awaken. The process consumes the

Dawning Echo and unlocks a new awakened Legendary Affix unique to the Awakened item. Only 1 item can be awakened per Legendary Gem and once an item is awakened, it will still stay that way forever. If you find any item upgrades, you can salvage the Awakened item at the Blacksmith to get your
Dawning Echo and Gems back to then awaken the new upgrade.

Cosmetics

The Awakening process also upgrades the item's appearance on your character and allows the item to gain Resonance.

These awakened Legendary Item effects can be very strong and in some cases build defining. Check out our Character Build Guides for more in-depth information on the individual classes.

Resonance

Once you have awakened a Legendary item with a Rank 10 Legendary Gem you can put more Legendary Gems into the item, boosting the item's Resonance + 1 per rank increasing your Items Base Attributes Life and Damage. Furthermore, you unlock special powers at Resonance level 10 (Primary Attack damage increased by 3%) and level 20 (Skill damage increased by 2%). These special powers are all the same for every 1-Star Legendary Gem:

A fully upgrade 1-Star Legendary Gem's Resonance Bonuses are:

  • Base Attribute Bonus increased by 20
  • Primary Attack damage increased by 3%
  • Skill damage increased by 2%
  • A New Character Appearance

All original attributes of the Legendary Gem used for the Resonance process do not matter. You can take out the Legendary Gems anytime at the Master Jeweler without losing them.

The type and quantity of socketed Resonant Gems depends on the item's primary Legendary Gem! The Resonance Bonus is active in PvP Combat and provides a massive advantage!

Summary

  • Obtain as many Legendary Gems as possible. Always prioritize Rare and
    Legendary Crest     as rewards.
  • Awakened Legendary Items have very powerful special effects.
  • Focus on Awakening a few 1-Star Legendary Gems first to unlock their special power.
  • The only way to awaken your items is to buy
    Eternal Orbs     in the shop.
  • Resonance is a decent bonus but should not be your primary focus to gain power.
