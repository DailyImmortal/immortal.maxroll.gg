Introduction

Legendary items provide bonuses to various skills and in some cases completely alter their behavior. Many of these items provide buffs to the same skill from different item slots, allowing you to stack power and decimate your foes. Check out our Equipment Guide and Set Items Guide for more informatin. Use the tabs below to see all available Legendary and Set Items for each class.
